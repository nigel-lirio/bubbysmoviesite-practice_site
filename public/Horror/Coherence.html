Coherence

Directed by: James Ward Byrkit

Synopsis: On the night of an astronomical anomaly, eight friends at a dinner party experience a series of reality-bending events that challenge their perceptions of each other and themselves. As the night unfolds, they must navigate a web of alternate realities and confront the consequences of their choices.

Review: "Coherence" is a mind-bending thriller that masterfully combines elements of science fiction and psychological drama. The film's low-budget approach is a testament to creative storytelling, relying heavily on improvisation and character dynamics to drive the plot. The ensemble cast delivers compelling performances, capturing the escalating tension and paranoia as the characters grapple with the surreal events unfolding around them. The narrative is intricately woven, keeping viewers on the edge of their seats as they piece together the puzzle of multiple realities. With its thought-provoking themes and unexpected twists, "Coherence" is a captivating exploration of identity, choice, and the nature of reality itself.
